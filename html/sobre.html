PRIYA PAINTINGS

True Work & Trustable

  Welcome to my professional portfolio, where you can explore a rich collection of my artistic works, including regional paintings that reflect cultural heritage, creatively crafted poster paintings, vibrant wall paintings that bring spaces to life, and book illustrations blending traditional and contemporary styles. Experience the timeless beauty of my wood paintings, each telling its own story, and discover a variety of techniques and mediums that showcase my years of dedication to the art of painting. This portfolio reflects my journey as an artist and educator, featuring collaborative projects with my students and highlighting my commitment to bringing tradition into modern contexts. Whether it’s regional art forms, public and private space adornments, campaign posters, or intricate book illustrations, each piece is crafted with attention to detail and a deep understanding of the medium, designed to inspire and provide insights into my artistic process. I invite you to explore and connect with the stories behind each artwork.
